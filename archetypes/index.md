<!-- Why These Domains Section -->
<section class="domains-explanation">
  <h2>Why These Six Domains?</h2>
  <p class="section-intro">These archetypes were selected based on critical knowledge gaps and coordination challenges revealed during recent pandemic responses, particularly COVID-19.</p>
  
  <div class="domains-grid">
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">🔬</div>
      <h3>The Scientific Foundation</h3>
      <p><strong>Biomedical & Physical Scientists</strong> provide fundamental understanding of pathogens, disease mechanisms, and medical countermeasures.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">🌿</div>
      <h3>The Natural Origins</h3>
      <p><strong>Ecologists</strong> help us understand that most pandemic threats emerge from disrupted human-animal-environment interfaces.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">👥</div>
      <h3>The Human Element</h3>
      <p><strong>Socio-behavioral Scientists</strong> recognize that human behavior ultimately determines intervention success or failure.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">💰</div>
      <h3>The Economic Reality</h3>
      <p><strong>Economists</strong> analyze massive financial disruptions and help balance health protection with economic sustainability.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">🏙️</div>
      <h3>The Physical Context</h3>
      <p><strong>Built Environment Specialists</strong> understand how infrastructure design facilitates or inhibits disease transmission.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="domain-card">
      <div class="domain-icon">📱</div>
      <h3>The Information Landscape</h3>
      <p><strong>Information Access Specialists</strong> navigate digital ecosystems where accurate information competes with misinformation.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
